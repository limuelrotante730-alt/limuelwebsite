📚 About the Fest

SMCC-CCIS Tech Fest 2026 is a week-long celebration of innovation and technology, bringing together students from the College of Computing and Information Sciences. The event features coding competitions, tech exhibits, interactive seminars, hands-on workshops, cybersecurity training, robotics demos, and student project presentations.

This event aims to inspire creativity, foster collaboration, and provide students with valuable industry insights and practical experience.
